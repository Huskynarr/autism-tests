About Autism Assessments

What is Autism Spectrum Disorder?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition characterized by differences in social communication and interaction, as well as restricted and repetitive patterns of behavior, interests, or activities.

Importance of Early Screening

Early identification and intervention can significantly improve outcomes for individuals with autism. These screening tools help identify potential signs that may warrant further professional evaluation.

⚠️ Important Disclaimer

These tools are for screening purposes only and do not provide a diagnosis. A comprehensive evaluation by qualified healthcare professionals is required for autism diagnosis.
